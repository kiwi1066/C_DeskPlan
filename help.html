Overview

The Desk Planner lets you visually assign desks to teams across the working week using an interactive floor plan. Add teams, allocate desks by day, compare days side-by-side, and export your plan as a CSV or image.

Your work is automatically saved to the browser after every change — you can close and reopen the tab without losing data. Use ↓ Export to create a portable backup file.

Basic Workflow

  1. Choose a Day from the day dropdown.
  2. Add teams via 👥 Import Teams (CSV) or ➕ Add Team.
  3. Select one or more desks on the floor plan.
  4. Click a team chip in the legend to assign the selected desks.
  5. Repeat for each day, or use 📋 Copy Day to duplicate a day across the week.
Tip: Hover over any coloured desk to see a tooltip with the team name.

Selecting Desks

Three ways to build a selection:

  • Single click — selects one desk and clears any previous selection.
  • Ctrl+click (or Cmd+click on Mac) — adds or removes a desk without clearing others.
  • 🤱 Multi ON — when active, every click adds to the selection automatically, no need to hold Ctrl.

Clicking an empty area of the floor plan clears the selection.

Shift+Drag Area Select

Hold Shift and drag across the floor plan to draw a selection rectangle. All desks whose area overlaps the rectangle are added to the selection on release.

  • The cursor changes to a crosshair when Shift is held, signalling that drag-select is ready.
  • Drag-select is always additive — existing selected desks are kept, so you can draw multiple regions.
  • Accidental micro-drags (under 6px) are ignored and treated as a normal click.
  • Drag-select is disabled in Compare mode.
Workflow: Use Shift+drag to select a zone roughly, then Ctrl+click to fine-tune individual desks.

Managing Teams

Adding teams

  • ➕ Add Team — creates one team at a time. Enter a display name when prompted. The ID (T1, T2…) is assigned automatically.
  • 👥 Import Teams — bulk-imports from a CSV file (see Import & Export).

Renaming a team

Hover over any team chip in the legend — a ✏️ edit button appears on the right edge. Click it to open the team editor. Edit the name and press Save or Enter. Press Escape to cancel.

The team's ID (e.g. T1) never changes — only the display name is updated.

Deleting a team

Open the team editor (✏️) and click Delete. If the team has existing desk assignments you will be offered two options:

  • Delete & Unassign — removes the team and clears all its desk colours across every day.
  • Delete, Keep Colours — removes the team from the legend but leaves desk colours in place, useful for gradual reassignment.

Assigning Desks to a Team

  1. Select one or more desks using any selection method.
  2. Click the team's coloured chip label in the Teams legend.
    (Click the label, not the ✏️ edit button.)
  3. Desk colours update instantly and the summary refreshes.

To unassign, select the desks and use 🧿 Clear Desks.

Toolbar Reference

File

👥 Import Teams Imports a two-column CSV (id,name). Replaces any existing teams.
📂 Import Restores a previously exported desk-plan CSV, including assignments and team names across all days.
↓ Export Exports the full desk plan to CSV. You will be prompted for a file name.
📸 Export Image Saves the current day's floor plan as a PNG with team colours and an allocation summary.

Edit

📋 Copy Day Copies all desk assignments from the current day to one or more other days selected via checkboxes.
↩ Undo Reverts the last desk-assignment change. Supports up to 50 undo steps per session.
🧿 Clear Desks Clears selected desks for the current day. If nothing is selected, clears the entire day.
🧹 Clear All Clears all desk assignments and removes all teams without reloading the page.
🗑 Reset Completely resets the application and reloads the page. All data is permanently lost.

Compare Mode

Switch the Mode dropdown to Compare to view two days side-by-side on the same floor plan. Choose which days to compare using the two day selectors that appear.

  • Desk editing and drag-select are disabled in Compare mode.
  • Switch back to Single to resume editing.

Desk Allocation Summary

The panel below the Teams legend updates in real time and shows:

  • Total — total desks on the floor.
  • Assigned — desks with a team for the current day.
  • Free — desks with no assignment.
  • A colour-coded count badge per team.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Shift + drag Draw a rubber-band rectangle to select multiple desks at once.
Ctrl + click  (Cmd on Mac) Add or remove a single desk from the current selection.
Enter  in team editor Save the team rename.
Escape  in team editor Close the team editor without saving.

Import & Export

Teams CSV format

Two columns with a header row. The ID must be T followed by a number.

id,name
T1,Finance
T2,Engineering
T3,HR

Desk plan CSV format

Generated by ↓ Export. One row per desk with team IDs for each day, plus team names as additional columns. Re-importable via 📂 Import.

desk,mon,tue,wed,thu,fri,mon_name,tue_name,wed_name,thu_name,fri_name
desk-001,T1,T1,T2,,T1,Finance,Finance,Engineering,,Finance
desk-002,T3,,,T3,,HR,,,HR,
Tip: The exported CSV includes team names alongside IDs, making it readable in Excel without needing to open the planner.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use Shift+drag for large zones, then Ctrl+click to fine-tune.
  • Use Copy Day to roll Monday's plan across the week, then adjust individual days.
  • Use Compare mode to review differences between two days before finalising.
  • Keep team IDs consistent across floors — they share the same colour scheme.
  • Use Delete, Keep Colours when retiring a team mid-week so the floor plan stays readable while you reassign desks.
  • Export regularly — the browser auto-save is convenient but a CSV backup lets you restore from any device.
